Hockey club Varna

The success of our ice hockey club is not just measured in championship wins, but in the personal growth and development of its members. Through their rigorous training programs, the club instills important life skills such as discipline, resilience, and teamwork, preparing players for success on and off the ice. This focus on individual growth and development is what sets the club apart and makes it a hero to the community.

About the club

  At Hockey Club Varna, we're passionate about the sport of ice hockey and the positive impact it can have on individuals and communities. Our mission is to develop skilled athletes and build strong, supportive communities through the power of hockey.
  Founded in 2019, Club Varna has been providing quality hockey programs to players of all ages and skill levels for over years. Our experienced coaching staff and supportive community of players and families created a welcoming and inclusive environment where everyone can learn and grow.

Teams

Men's team

 Men's team of our club has regular ice hockey tranining, participates annually in the Bulgarian National Hockey Championship (BAHL), and it is the champion of BAHL's last season 2022 / 2023, also we take part in various hockey tournaments and we're a champion of the international, Hockey Tournament "Golden Black Sea Cup" - 2022. Players from our club are also regularly included in the Bulgarian national team.

Children's team

   For the first time ever. in 11-th and 12-th of March 2023, the children's team of Ice Hockey Club "VARNA" played two difficult and spectacular hockey matches in Sofia.
